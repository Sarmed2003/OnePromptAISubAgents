# Swarm lane 17: Historian Agent

## Overview

Lane 17 is the Historian agent responsible for narrating museum exhibition timelines. This agent consumes age data from lane 16 and provenance information from lane 15 to construct a coherent chronological narrative that highlights significant specimens and tells the story of the exhibition's historical arc.

## Purpose

The Historian agent transforms raw specimen data into engaging exhibition narratives that contextualize artifacts within their historical periods and significance. The output, `exhibition_narrative`, provides a structured story with temporal progression and specimen highlights that make exhibitions come alive for visitors and educators.

## Input Dependencies

- **Lane 16 (Age Data)**: Provides temporal information about specimens, including dating methods, age ranges, and chronological context.
- **Lane 15 (Provenance)**: Supplies the historical provenance, acquisition history, and archaeological/historical background of each specimen.

## Output

**exhibition_narrative**: A chronological narrative document containing:
- Thematic story arc organized by historical periods
- Specimen highlights with contextual descriptions
- Temporal markers and connections between artifacts
- Narrative threads that connect disparate objects into a coherent exhibition experience

## Downstream Consumers

- **Lane 18 (Educator)**: Uses the exhibition narrative to generate quiz content and educational materials for different visitor demographics.
- **Lane 28 (Manager)**: Uses the narrative structure and specimen highlights to plan tour routes, timing, and visitor flow through the exhibition.

## Narrative Construction Strategy

The Historian agent:
1. Organizes specimens chronologically using age data from lane 16
2. Enriches the timeline with provenance context from lane 15
3. Identifies thematic connections across time periods
4. Highlights pivotal specimens that mark transitions or illustrate key historical moments
5. Constructs a compelling story arc that guides visitors through the exhibition
6. Generates summaries of each historical period represented in the collection

## Implementation Notes

The Historian agent must balance scholarly accuracy with narrative engagement, ensuring that the exhibition narrative is both historically sound and accessible to diverse museum audiences. The output format should support flexible consumption by downstream agents with different requirements and presentation styles.
